About me

I am a 3rd-year PhD candidate in the statistics department of CREST, Institut Polytechnique de Paris , under the supervision of Cristina Butucea.

My research delves into the foundational aspects of mathematical statistics and machine learning, placing a special emphasis on crafting practical and efficient learning methods. I am particularly interested in time series, random matrix theory and high dimensional estimation problems.

In addition to my research activites, I serve as a teaching assistant at ENSAE.
